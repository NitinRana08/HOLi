Holika Dahan 🔥

Holika Dahan is a sacred ritual celebrated on the night before Holi. A bonfire is lit to symbolize the victory of good over evil.

The tradition comes from the story of Prahlad, a devoted follower who remained faithful despite facing danger. Evil tried to destroy him through fire, but divine protection saved him while negativity was destroyed.

The bonfire represents burning away ego, hatred, and negativity, reminding us that truth and righteousness always triumph.
